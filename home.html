Do you know there are four types of social bubble?





Back to Main

Audience Measurement Bubble:

Designing algorithms to do directional push to audiences based on their needs, beliefs and attitudes

Ranking Algorithm Bubble:

Algorithms do not rank news in the same way in terms of content and length, and people remember the information they come across first

Influencers Bubble:

People will follow influential people on social media, leading to cognitive bias

Big Data bubble:

Algorithm applies pressure on users, puts users in a framework and eventually decides on our future behaviors
